The Retail Advantage: Fact vs. Reasoning

Why microDOS(2) outperforms organic mushroom products on retail shelves.

Zero Dosage Variance

The Fact: Every pill contains exactly 2mg of 4-HO-MET.

The Why: Organic mushrooms have natural variations in alkaloid content (psilocybin/psilocin) from batch to batch, and even from cap to stem. This causes unpredictable results for the end-user. Our synthetic pressing eliminates this variance, drastically reducing customer complaints regarding efficacy and ensuring repeat purchases.

Elimination of Nausea

The Fact: The formula is clean and causes no gastric distress.

The Why: Organic mushrooms contain chitin, a fibrous substance in the cell walls that human stomachs lack the enzyme (chitinase) to easily break down. This is the primary cause of the nausea associated with traditional shrooms. By using isolated 4-HO-MET, we bypass chitin digestion entirely.

Rapid 10-Minute Onset

The Fact: Cognitive effects begin in approximately 10 minutes.

The Why: Traditional psilocybin must be metabolized by the liver into psilocin before crossing the blood-brain barrier, which takes 45-60 minutes. Our synthesized compound is formulated for rapid absorption, providing immediate clarity and a highly predictable session window for the consumer.
